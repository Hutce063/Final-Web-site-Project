What is fantasy?

Fantasy is what we belive. Such as dragons, fairies, supernatural beings, and many other things. The main point is that fantasy means things that don't exist in this world. It can lead to kids not getting bored waiting on something, they are using their imagination to entertain themselves. Have you ever had an imaginary friend.     Orgins of Fantasy

    Ninetheenth Century is where modern fantasy began following the period of chivalrous European romances and tales whose fantastical elements were still considered somewhat believable. There was a scottish author named George MacDonald, whose noval Phantastes (1858) featured a young man who was drawn into a dream world where he has a series of adventures. That book is credited with the writing the first plainly fictitious fantasy for adults. The book that set to genre fantasy. The book is called The Well at the World's End(1896) written by Englishman William Morris. This book set the fantasy genre into effect because he had created a whole entire world that existed beyond the known world.

    Since them Fantasy has grown im popularity. Just think for a minute. Where would we be without J.K Rowling's Harry Potter novels (1997-2007)?
